What is MEGAHERB?
MEGAHERB is a personal music project pushing the sonic boundaries of sound design, rhythm, texture, and tonality.

MEGAHERB encompasses all my audio-based creative output, including music production and development, as well as visual aesthetic. It is a perpetual WIP in constant evolution.

Projects and Achievements
- Construction of ultrasonic microphones for recording audio outside of the human hearing range for sound design purposes. This involves soldering microphone capsules with an optional PCB for use with a field recording device. These samples can be slowed down to reshape the perceived size of recorded objects and atmospheres while retaining a full audio spectrum.

- Creation of a track within an 18 hour time limit for the Form Any% Level 2 compilation. My track "Cataclysm" was voted to be selected for the compilation among 325 total entries. Cataclysm explores modern bass music sound design and spatial warping effects. [LINK]

- Demonstration track for a purchasable sample pack. [LINK]

- Ambient track "Solstice" explores various sound design techniques manipulating vocals and recorded textures, submitted for a compilation with the requirement of using plugins from the hosting developer. [LINK]

- Started work on plugin development, currently with the Apple Audio Unit framework in C++. Exploring proper C++ practices and design patterns, and studying efficient utilisation of modern processors.

- Beta tested audio plugins and software for the following companies and developers: Ableton, apulSoft, Ewan Bristow, blepfx, Anukari, Sonic Academy, LHI Audio, Polyverse Music, u-He. Personal vouches from various developers can be provided upon request!
